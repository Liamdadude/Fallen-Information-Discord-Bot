Barry Case File

Gender: male 
species/race: elf
Personality: he is very shy. He is not good at handling his emotions and he will often have random breakdowns with no warning at all. He is very paranoid and somewhat naive. He is not very aggressive and tends to avoid fighting even if he gets attacked. He is also very loyal and will do anything for a friend. 
Other stuff: his closest friend is ryan. He is actually heir to the throne in Hetherston but Nix and Ryan don't know that. His father is king bond but Barry is adopted. He is one of the very few survivors of the cindervil fire, none of his family survived.  He is helpless and terrified of being alone.
